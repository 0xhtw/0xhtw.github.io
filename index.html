// htw.md [ README ]

HTW stands for Hack The World — began in Milton Keynes, a city with a reputation for pioneering tech and bold urban design. What started as a small artistic collective evolved into a small group of people passionate about creativity, technology and exploration in both urban and digital senses.

HTW exists across different spaces, both online and offline. Representing our crossover between urban street art culture and technology. It's more than a crew, it's a mindset of exploration, experimentation, and pushing boundaries.

That philosophy guides my work in cybersecurity: the drive to learn, to innovate and share knowledge in ways that make systems stronger and uncover new findings in the forever evolving world of cybersecurity.
